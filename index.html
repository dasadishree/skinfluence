ABOUT US

At Skinfluence LI, we believe everyone deserves access to quality skincare and the knowledge to care for their skin and the field of dermatology. Founded by 2 best friends, the primary prupose of our organization is to spread awareness and to provide our community with accessibility to dermatology products. We plan on incorporating local fundraising and volunteering events that can support our community.
